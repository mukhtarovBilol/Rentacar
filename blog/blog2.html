Traveling around Baku

The capital city of Azerbaijan, Baku impresses with its rich history, diverse culture and fascinating attractions. Exploring this unique city reveals many amazing beauties and interesting stories to visitors. Let’s dive into the magical world of Baku and explore its most striking and amazing attractions.

1. Old Town - Icheri Sheher
The Old Town of Baku, also known as Icheri Sheher, is one of the most ancient and characteristic places in the city. Its narrow winding streets, stone walls and architectural masterpieces attract many tourists and explorers. In Icheri Sheher, you can go on a fascinating journey into the past, exploring its ancient mosques, caravanserais and historical monuments. One of the most famous sites in Icheri Sheher is the Maiden Tower, the symbol of the city that adorns the local flag.

2. Maidan Tower
The Maidan Tower, also known as the Giz Galasi Tower, is a magnificent architectural marvel of modern Baku. This skyscraper rises above the city and the Caspian Sea, creating a stunning contrast to the historic center of Baku. The Maidan Tower has become a symbol of Azerbaijan’s prosperity and modern development, and its height exceeds 180 meters. Visitors can enjoy breathtaking views of the city and the coast from the observation deck at the top of the tower.

3. Tezeh Pir Mosque
The Tezeh Pir Mosque is one of the most famous and beautiful religious buildings in Baku. With its graceful arched forms and detailed decoration, it attracts both believers and architecture lovers. The Tezeh Pir Mosque was built in 1914 and has since served as a place of worship and prayer for the Muslims of Baku.

4. National History Museum of Azerbaijan
Located in the heart of Baku, the National History Museum of Azerbaijan is a treasure trove of knowledge about the country's rich history and culture. The museum features unique archaeological finds, art, household items and historical artifacts that allow you to immerse yourself in the history of Azerbaijan from ancient times to the present day.

5. Flamingo Park
Flamingo Park is an amazing oasis of nature and tranquility in the center of Baku. Its green lawns, picturesque ponds and blooming gardens attract many locals and tourists, and offer an ideal place for strolls, picnics and relaxation from the bustle of the city.

6. Upland Park Island
Upland Park Island is one of the most popular places for entertainment and recreation in Baku. Its beautiful parks, attractions and entertainment centers attract families, friends and tourists, and create a unique atmosphere of fun and joy.

7. Fountain Square
Fountain Square is the heart of Baku's nightlife, where locals and tourists gather to enjoy music, light shows and a wide range of restaurants and cafes. Its magnificent fountains and architectural complexes create a unique atmosphere of fun and celebration.

8. Museum of Modern Art
The Museum of Modern Art is a place where you can enjoy the works of contemporary artists and sculptors from Azerbaijan and around the world. Its exhibitions and collections offer a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in the world of modern

art and appreciate its diversity and beauty.

9. Ancient City Aquapark
Ancient City Aquapark is a place where you can enjoy fun and entertainment for the whole family. Its pools, slides and attractions offer a great opportunity to spend time outdoors and get a boost of energy and positive emotions.

10. Baku Boulevard
Baku Boulevard is a beautiful promenade that stretches along the Caspian Sea and offers beautiful views of the water and the city. Its cozy squares, street cafes and restaurants create a unique atmosphere and attract many tourists and locals.

Baku is an amazing city that is full of beauty and amazing discoveries. Its sights and atmosphere leave an unforgettable impression on everyone who visits it. Being the capital and cultural center of Azerbaijan, Baku is a place that is definitely worth visiting and exploring.
