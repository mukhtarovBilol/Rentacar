Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements for renting a car?

To rent a car, you must be at least 24 years old, and your driving experience with a valid driver's license should be more than 2 years.

Can foreign nationals rent a car?

Yes, foreign nationals can rent a car by presenting their passport and driver's license. As per Azerbaijani law, foreign drivers are permitted to use their foreign driver's license for up to one month after entering the country. If their stay exceeds one month, they may face fines, and the vehicle could be impounded. Other rental conditions remain the same as outlined in the general terms.

Is a deposit required for car rental?

Yes, a deposit is typically required, depending on the car and rental duration. The minimum deposit starts at 100 AZN.

What documents are needed to rent a car?

For citizens of Azerbaijan, a valid ID and driver's license are required to complete the rental process.
